About me

A registered massage therapist regulated through the College of Massage Therapist of Ontario (Canada) since 2016 with 5 years of experience working in a physiotherapy environment. I have had the pleasure of seeing and treating an array physical dysfunctions and limitations, working hand in hand with chiropractors, kinesiologists, personal trainers, physicians and physiotherapists in a multidisciplinary healthcare network of rehabilitation to help people achieve their goals in pain free activity and living.


Areas of focus include but are not limited to:


  • Deep Tissue Massage
  • Myofascial Release
  • Motor Vehicle Accidents (MVA)
  • Workplace Injuires (WSIB)
  • Post-op Rehabilitation
  • Sports Injuries
